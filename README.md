# Tech for Tomorrow

Tech for Tomorrow is a sustainability-focused web project created by Team Grapes of Wrath. The site explores how technology can both support and harm environmental sustainability, helping visitors think critically about responsible innovation and everyday digital choices.

## Project Focus

- Title: Tech for Tomorrow: Understanding the Relationship Between Sustainability and Technology
- Core idea: Technology is not only the solution and not only the problem
- Goal: Encourage informed, balanced decisions about digital habits and sustainability

## Audience

- College students
- Young professionals
- Consumers interested in sustainability
- Educators
- Community members seeking environmental information

## Website Sections

- Home overview (problem statement and purpose)
- Technology Helping the Environment
- The Hidden Environmental Costs
- Artificial Intelligence and Sustainability (benefits and challenges)
- Embedded real-world case study: Costs of AI
- Interactive Carbon Footprint Calculator
- Sustainable Technology Guide
- Future Innovations
- Outcome and key takeaways

## Interactive Feature

The calculator estimates a user’s monthly digital lifestyle footprint using:

- Device ownership
- Streaming habits
- Online shopping frequency
- Transportation mode and weekly mileage

It returns an estimated impact level and practical recommendations.
